1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account information: name, email address, password (encrypted)
  • Profile data: age, gender, fitness goals, health metrics
  • Device information: device type, model, operating system version
  • Communication: if you contact us, we may keep records of your inquiries and responses

1.2 Health and Fitness Data (Garmin Integration)

When you connect your Garmin account or device to our App, we collect activity and health data from your Garmin account. This data is obtained directly through your authorization and includes:

  • Activity data: steps, distance, calories burned, duration, date, time, activity type, training load, training effect
  • Health metrics: heart rate (average, maximum, and variability), sleep data (duration, stages, quality), stress levels, body metrics (weight, body fat percentage)
  • Performance metrics: pace, speed, power output, cadence, elevation gain, VO2 max, estimated workload, personal records
  • Training data: structured workouts, training plans, goals, fitness trends, training status
  • Location data: GPS coordinates and route information (only from activities you recorded with location enabled on your Garmin device)
  • Device information: Garmin device type, device serial number, device name, software version

Important regarding health data: We do not sell, trade, or use your health and fitness data for advertising, marketing, or any purpose unrelated to improving your experience with our App. We do not share this data with third parties for commercial purposes without your explicit consent.

1.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device identifiers: unique device ID, advertising ID
  • Usage data: features accessed, app interactions, crash logs
  • Analytics: analytics tools to understand app performance and user behavior
  • IP address and network information

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. The retention period depends on the type of data:

  • Account information: Retained while your account is active. Upon account deletion, deleted within 30 days.
  • Activity and health data: Retained while your account is active and your Garmin integration is connected. If you disconnect Garmin integration, we will retain the previously synchronized data as part of your App history unless you request deletion.
  • Backup and system logs: Retained for up to 60 days after deletion for backup and security purposes.
  • Analytics and aggregated data: Retained indefinitely as it is de-identified and cannot be linked to you.

When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law. However, please note:

  • Any data you shared publicly or with other users may remain visible
  • Your Garmin account and its data will only be affected if you disconnect the integration
  • Garmin's own data retention policies apply to your Garmin account; contact Garmin directly for more information about deleting data from their servers

12. Garmin Integration and Data Transfer

Our App integrates with Garmin Connect through Garmin's official APIs. When you authorize our App to access your Garmin account, the following applies:

12.1 Data Access and Transfer

  • You grant us express authorization to access your Garmin Connect account and retrieve activity and health data
  • Your data is retrieved directly from Garmin's servers when you sync, or through Garmin's push notifications to our servers
  • We store a local copy of your synced data on our servers to provide features like historical analysis, trends, and insights
  • Data transfers between your device, Garmin's servers, and our servers are encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols

12.2 Data Use Restrictions

  • We do not resell your Garmin data to third parties for commercial purposes
  • We do not use your health or activity data for targeted advertising
  • We do not share your data with data brokers or marketing networks
  • We do not train commercial AI models on your personal health data without explicit consent

12.3 Revoking Access

You can disconnect your Garmin account from our App at any time through:

  • Our App settings: Go to Settings → Connected Accounts → Disconnect Garmin
  • Garmin Connect: Revoke access by removing our App from your Garmin account's authorized applications

When you disconnect, we will stop retrieving new data from Garmin. However, previously synchronized data will remain in your App account unless you request deletion.

12.4 Garmin's Privacy

For information about how Garmin handles your data, please review Garmin's Privacy Policy. We comply with Garmin's Developer Program Agreement and data protection requirements. By connecting your Garmin account, you acknowledge that you have reviewed Garmin's privacy practices and consent to our use of the data in accordance with this Policy.
